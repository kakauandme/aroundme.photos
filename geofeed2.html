FAQ

What is Geofeedia?

Geofeedia is a location-based social media monitoring, analysis and engagement platform. Businesses, media companies, and government agencies use Geofeedia to understand social media happening at locations important to them. Geofeedia’s patented technologies allow you to search and monitor areas as large as a city or as small as a single building.

Which social media services does Geofeedia search?

Geofeedia currently searches Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, Picasa, Flickr, and Viddy.

How is Geofeedia different from traditional social media listening tools such as Radian6, BrandWatch, or Sysomos?

Geofeedia searches by location first rather than by a specific series of words (such as keywords or hashtags). The net result: Geofeedia’s data set contains the hidden 70%+ of data coming from locations compared to traditional tools that rely on certain words to be included in their data sets.

Hotels use Geofeedia to improve guest services, support crisis management, complement loyalty programs, identify key influencers, supply content marketing efforts and target promotional programs.

Restaurants use Geofeedia to improve diner satisfaction, monitor in-restaurant sentiment, inform menu composition, identify key influencers, improve staff performance and gain competitive insight.

Entertainment Companies use Geofeedia to monitor sentiment, improve promotional effectiveness, enable targeted sponsor engagement, support influencer-marketing programs and inform distribution channel effectiveness.

Consumer Brands use Geofeedia as to complement their integrated marketing mix, increase effectiveness of promotional events, target crisis management, improve customer service, inform product development, support influencer marketing initiatives, analyze channel effectiveness and streamline multi-cultural marketing programs.

Destination Marketers use Geofeedia to engage with visitors, monitor special events, acquire brand assets to re-publish and drive traffic to venues.

Event Marketers use Geofeedia to monitor events, understand the conversation, what attendees found most interesting, present special offers during the event and more.

Media Companies use Geofeedia to identify breaking stories, tap into citizen journalist generated images, find sources, gauge on-scene sentiment, corroborate facts, investigate stories and procure hyper-local content.

Public Safety Agencies use Geofeedia to respond to emergency situations, gain situational awareness, investigate criminal activity, support community policing and improve security operations.

Why would I use Geofeedia in addition to traditional social listening tools?

Geofeedia takes a unique perspective on social listening: location first. Other tools start with keyword and hashtag searches first to aggregate and analyze large amounts of data, typically without regard to geography as the primary filter. Geofeedia’s location-first approach gathers highly contextual data directly from the locations that are important to you.

Every day social media users are posting important content from your locations without using the “right” keywords and hashtags. Geofeedia helps you gather and analyze this meaningful data quickly and efficiently.

Aside from finding data, how else does Geofeedia help me do my job?

Geofeedia has great tools to help you with your workflow once you’ve found the data you’ve been looking for:

  • Filter by date, source, time, and keyword.
  • Save the parameters of your search as a “Geofeed” for future review.
  • Curate important posts you find as “Collections”.
  • Share Geofeeds, posts, and collections with co-workers via email, social media or our API.

Why can’t I see some data in Geofeedia?

Geofeedia finds data that is publicly available and can be identified by exact location. Social media posters using GPS-enabled devices generate most of Geofeedia’s content. All social media services, and most GPS enabled devices, have privacy settings that allow users to turn off geo-location, and certain social media services permit the user to define their posts as private. Private data, or data without an identified location, will not be visible to you in Geofeedia.

How long does it take for data to show up on Geofeedia?

In most cases, content shows up within seconds of the original posting.

Are you adding other social media data sources?

Yes! Geofeedia’s customers have been very pleased with the data provided from Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, Flickr, Picasa, YouTube and Viddy. As an open-source platform, Geofeedia will constantly be on the lookout for other data sources that our customers deem important.

Can I download data from Geofeedia?

Yes! Saved Geofeeds and collections from monitored locations can be accessed via RSS for syndication and export. The data you find can also be downloaded in a CSV format.

What about privacy concerns?

Geofeedia only gathers information that is publicly available and in compliance with our social media partners’ terms of service. All of Geofeedia’s social media partners allow users to restrict access to their posts and deactivate geo-location features.
